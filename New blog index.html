The Describe Journey I Have Taken – Road Trip to Ooty

Last month, I went on my first road trip with my family to Ooty. It was a bright sunny morning when we started our journey. I was very excited because it was my first long trip in a car, and the day was filled with happiness. We started the car from our home early in the morning, and we enjoyed the trip by listening to songs and singing together. I felt so joyful because everyone was happy and enjoying the moment.

After travelling nearly 15 km, my dad stopped the car near a hotel for breakfast. We had hot idly and dosa, and the food tasted very good. After breakfast, we continued our journey on the ECR road around 11:00 AM. The weather was perfect—not too sunny and not too cold. The cool wind through the car window made the journey refreshing. Suddenly, I asked my dad to stop the car so that we could take photos. I clicked many pictures with my family, and I also took some selfies which I uploaded on my Instagram story. After that, we started travelling again towards Ooty.

We travelled almost 350 km on the first day. Finally, we reached our hotel at around 10 PM. The hotel name was The King, and it was a very pleasant and clean place. From the hotel, Ooty was nearly 70 km away. The hotel staff were very friendly and took good care of us like their own family. After a good dinner, we went to sleep because we were tired from travelling.

The next morning, we woke up early, had hot coffee and breakfast, and started travelling again. On the way, I saw beautiful hills and thick mist covering the mountains like a white blanket. I also heard that the Ooty hill road has 69 hairpin bends, and it was scary but exciting. The view was amazing, and I enjoyed seeing fresh carrots and vegetables being sold by local people. We bought chocolates, homemade cake and tea powder. The local people were very kind and spoke politely, which made me feel happy.

After travelling the whole day, we returned back to our hotel around 4:30 PM in the evening. We were so tired that we couldn’t explore more places, so we decided to rest. My brother said that he cannot travel anymore because he was exhausted. We took a short nap, and after sleeping we felt fresh and energetic again.

The next day, we visited many beautiful places such as the Botanical Garden, Doddabetta Peak and Ooty Lake. The view from Doddabetta felt like we were standing above the clouds. Boating in the lake was peaceful and enjoyable. We took many photos and created wonderful memories.

Impact of This Journey

This journey created a big impact on my life. It was not just a fun trip—it changed the way I think. I realised the importance of spending time with family. Nowadays everyone is busy with phones and work, but during this trip, we talked, laughed, sang songs and enjoyed every moment together. I understood that real happiness lies in small moments, not expensive things.

This trip made me appreciate nature more. The mountains, hills, cold weather and waterfalls made my heart peaceful. Nature taught me to be calm and kind. I also learned courage. Even though the 69 bends road was scary, we still enjoyed it. It taught me that life also has twists and turns, but we must move forward with confidence.

Meeting new people made me understand the value of kindness. The hardworking shopkeepers and hotel staff always smiled and treated us with love. I learned to be thankful for what I have and stop complaining.

This journey gave me new dreams. Now I want to travel more places and learn more about the world. I feel more positive, confident and stronger after this experience. This Ooty trip was unforgettable. It became a beautiful memory that will stay in my heart forever.

“The best journeys are the ones that bring us closer to our loved ones and closer to ourselves.”

Challenges and Moments of Uncertainty

The 69 hairpin bends scared all of us. I was worried during each turn. My brother was tired and wanted to stop. We didn’t know if we would reach safely at night.

  • Face challenges step by step
